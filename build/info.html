Info
You can find information about our events on our social media such as our Instagram and Discord.
You will need to be a member to join our in-person events, although everyone is welcome to join events in the Discord server!
Anyone can be verified to our server as long as you go through the verification process correctly. You do not have to be a member to participate in server activities!
Being a member will allow you to attend all our in-person events while also getting membership discounts with some of our sponsors.
Easiest way is to come to an in-person event and we can walk you through the process on the spot as well as give you your AUNSW membership card.
If there are any grave concerns or complaints, please contact us at unswanime@gmail.com.

Want to contact us?

General Enquiries

For all general questions and information, please don't hesitate to reach out. Simply send an email to unswanime@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can. Alternatively, feel free to reach out on any of our socials, which can be found in the top right corner of the website under the “Join” header.

Sponsorship Enquiries

Interested in collaborating with AUNSW as a sponsor? We welcome partnerships that can enhance the experiences of our anime community. Please direct all sponsorship-related queries to our externals director Felix at sponsors@animeunsw.net, and let's explore how we can work together.
